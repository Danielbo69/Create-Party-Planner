My name is Jessica Rua, Benji’s mom, whose been the greatest inspiration and motivation to improve ourselves as a family. This whole adventure began back in 2021 when my son was diagnosed with autism spectrum disorder. From that moment our lives changed and the need for a more flexible schedule and being able to not only support my children and their needs but also being productive became a priority. Learning along the way with my son at each stage in this journey has brought us together as a family.

In this constant struggle with sensory and motor skills development in each of Benji’s therapy sessions, Creative Party Planner stopped being a dream and became a reality, a company that cares for our customers by providing a professional service with care and love while helping the development of motor and sensory skills of children with fun and creativity, taking care of every detail during any event or celebration so everyone can enjoy themselves.
