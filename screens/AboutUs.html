Our friendship began with our children. In 2021 both were diagnosed with autism spectrum disorder, from that moment our need to be owners of our time began in order to accompany and share each stage of development and growth.

In the constant struggle with the development of sensory and motor skills of our children, we came up with the idea, an idea that is now a dream called "Brandon Luxury events" where we combine fun with the development of motor and sensory skills.

Our children were the motivation behind making magic possible and awakening in us the same passion where we put all our love and creativity to make your event the most fun, unique, safe, clean, and elegant event. Taking care of every detail and offering the best service possible to our customers.
