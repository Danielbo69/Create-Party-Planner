What is Balloons Decorations?

• They are techniques used by balloons artists to obtain magnificent figures (arches, garlands, flowers, caricatures...) that adapt and create decorative, colorful environments in a unique, luxurious and original way.

What is Soft Play?

• It's an interactive play area that can be installed inside/outside the house or private event, designed to create fun and allow the little ones to play safely and cleanly. It's made of foam, it is soft and delicate for children to enjoy, explore and develop motor, sensory and social skills.

What are your rules for the playing area?

• No shoes.

• Painted faces are not allowed.

• Gum, food, drinks are not allowed.

• Children over 5 years old are not allowed to enter.

• Diapers can't be changed inside the playing area.

• A parent should always be supervising their child at all times.

• The balls must always remain inside the ball pool and all our games must be inside the area.

• A fee will be charged if these rules are not followed

What are the advantages of soft play?

• Interaction with more children.

• Imagination development.

• Encourages creative play.

• Offers fun and entertainment.

• Development of motor, sensory and social skills..

What time do you arrive prior to the event?

• For either balloon decorations or rental of softplay we usually arrive 2 to 4 hours before the event.

Do you desinfect soft play equipment before each event?

• All equipment is desinfected after each event, including the balls. We use non-toxic products and then clean with 100% pure alcohol disinfectant wipes to remove all bacteria/viruses.

Do you use shine for balloons in indoor or outdoor installations?

• Balloon glow/shine will only be added if set up completely under shade.

What if it rains?

• If you hold your event outdoors, you are responsible for changes in the weather.

For the safety of the little ones, the play areas and the balloon decoration will not be installed if there is rain forecast. It is always recommended to have an interior space as a second option. However, you can reschedule your event if you wish, based on availability.
